Smoking

Findings shows that most tobacco contains a substance called Nicotine which is an addictive substance, which causes the body system to experience physical withrawal symptoms and cravings. It causes rise in heart rate, blood pressure and blocks the arteries over time, all of this are contributing factors to your health life.However, you can still fix this that is why we are here to get you back to a healthy and fulling life. Our wellness coaching journey is never about telling telling our clients what to do, but respecting your terms and your reasons for "wanting or not wanting" to change. It is our job to show up for you in whatever way you need. Here are some few things that can be an obstacle in achieving your health goals and wellness potentials.

Stress

As we know it is a great enemy to our entire body structure and function, physically and mentally it influences our mood by inviting Anxiety, Restlessness, Feeling unmotivated or losing focus and being overwhelmed with every happenings around us. Stress makes us overeact, Angry and may lead to Drug or alchol misuse and tobacco. It may feel like your world is crumbling down right in front of you, do not panic, We can help you rectify this.

Nutrition

Eating right and eating healthy is one of the most effectice ways of preventing chronic diseases, eating healthy is key to your health. A healthy diet lets your body function it's full capacity taking you far away from chances of Obesity, Heart disease ,Diabetes, complications etc. It is not over if you begin a journey to the right direction regarding your Diet.

Sleep

Take your mobile phone battery as typical example, when it is drained, what do we do? we rescue the situation by recharging our phone so that we could continue using it, this same thing applies to our body when we are exhausted , we need a recharge and we can only achieve this through sleep, this is the only means of letting the body repair, heal itself from stress, there are varieties of things we do consciously and unconsciously which hinders our sleeping patterns and ability to sleep. Some of this shall be communicated to you in a lay man's terms and you get your sleep back on track by following this instructions.

Getting you Active

Regular physical activity can improve your muscle strength and boost your endurance. Exercise delivers oxygen and nutrients to your tissues and helps your cardiovascular system work more efficiently. And when your heart and lung health improve, you have more energy to tackle daily chores.We are here to put you on the right simple but beneficial activities that will improve your overall body functions.

Time management

This is the process of planning and exercising conscious control of time spent on specific activities, especially to increase effectiveness, efficiency and productivity. Using your time effectively gives you the option on spending or managing activities at your own time and expediency. Our time management instructions are detailed about how to achieve this without breaking a bank.
